Co Habit App

Co-habit is an app concept I designed to help roommates better manage shared living responsibilities and maintain a balanced household. The app focuses on improving communication, organizing tasks, and ensuring fairness among roommates. With features such as chore assignment, expense tracking, and event coordination, Co-habit provides a simple and efficient way to regulate daily responsibilities and avoid conflicts.

As part of this project, I created fully functional wireframes to demonstrate the user experience and flow of the app. These wireframes highlight the core features, including a chore board with assigned tasks, an expense tracker for shared bills, and a notifications system to keep roommates updated. The goal of Co-habit is to make co-living more manageable, transparent, and stress-free by integrating everyday responsibilities into one easy-to-use platform.

  • Chore assignment and tracking
  • Expense tracking for shared bills
  • Event coordination for roommates
  • Notification system for updates
  • Wireframes created in Figma
